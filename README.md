## Featured Project

### LMIA Job Highlighter

![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/status-in%20development-F59E0B?style=flat-square)
![Chrome Extension](https://img.shields.io/badge/platform-Chrome%20Extension-4285F4?style=flat-square&logo=googlechrome&logoColor=white)

A Chrome extension that helps job seekers identify companies with approved LMIA
positions directly on LinkedIn, Indeed, and Jobright. It synchronizes company
information from Google Sheets, matches legal company names with job-site
listings, and displays clear badges on job postings.

**Built with:** TypeScript, React, Vite, Tailwind CSS, Chrome Extension APIs,
and Vitest.
